Stumbled upon https://decoding.io/2021/05/refactoring-my-gtd-system-part-2-the-capture-wallet/ today and it led me down the path of:

Have Capture Tools everywhere.

I’ve found on a number of occasions I have a good idea or at least something that keeps me from sleeping and being able to get it out of my head and on to paper would be preferred.

According to David Allen you can’t keep more than 4 things in your head without diminishing your cognitive ability. You mind is deceptive and you think you will remember, but you won’t. There is now a notebook by the bedside. I just need to put a few in the vehicles and I hopefully should be set.

Came across this: https://capturewallet.com/. Too expensive, but interesting.
