60-30-10 Color Rule

Published March 31, 2022

Proposes that your color usage should break down to 60% of Viewport is in your neutral color 30% is in your brand or primary color 10% is in your accent/call-to-action color

For Dorsata our neutral is the grey background 30% is our white area’s we’re asking folks to focus their attention on 10% is our deep green for actionable areas.
