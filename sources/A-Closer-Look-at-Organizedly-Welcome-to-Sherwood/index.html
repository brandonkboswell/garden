Highlights

Projects. These are items that are brought together to help achieve a specific goal by a specific date. Notes and tasks can belong to a project. Projects become completed. Areas (known as Tags in Organizedly). These are notes that have related content. In Organizedly, you use tags to group your notes into areas. In this way, notes can belong to a project and to more than one area. Resources. (Also called Collections.) Groups of related notes that you want to continue to have access to regardless of project status. Some people call these evergreen notes. Archive. The place to stow away completed project information on the chance you may want to reference them again in the future, but that are not part of your current work load.
