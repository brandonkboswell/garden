Get your reps in
November 29, 2023

You have to do a bunch of shitty work before you will do good work. It’s about getting your reps in, and there really aren’t shortcuts. This idea was taken from an interview on The Ground Up Show. I thought it was especially interesting. We often don’t do things for fear of them being bad, but if we change our mindset to “at the beginning it will be bad, we just need to work through the bad period,” then it’s much easier to get started. Move forward with decisions that are easily adjusted later
October 15, 2023

Significant time is lost over-discussing choices that can be easily adjusted later. If it can be changed painlessly later, choose what appears to be a decent option and revisit this in a retrospective where you’ll have more information.
