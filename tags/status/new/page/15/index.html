Don’t do everything

May 23, 2022

Unless a task is urgent, it is helpful to have some time away from the idea to evaluate whether it is truly as important as it felt when capturing it. Often times the answer is no. So the point isn’t to eradicate procrastination, but to choose more wisely what you’re going to procrastinate on, in order to focus on what matters most.
