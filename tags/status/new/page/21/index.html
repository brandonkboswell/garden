Wordle
March 28, 2022

Wordle has been great as it has reconnected me with my best friend. Wordle has given us a connection point each day when we chat over text message. Two key aspects are that there is only one each day and that everyone is working on the same one. The limited manner gives each guess more weight and also ensures people don’t binge it and grow tired of it. The fact everyone is working on the same puzzle is what adds to the social element of the game.
