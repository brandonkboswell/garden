Unlike a list of stuff I have to do, it’s a brain dump of tasks I might do if I feel like it. I try to capture 100% of the possible tasks for that day. This clears my brain of internal clutter and helps The Pilot confirm that the Daily Highlight is the highest-priority thing for the day. In my case, I try to put my internal clutter in TickTick and keep it away from my Daily Highlight for fear that too many intentions for the day may distract from my real focus for today (the Daily Highlight). April 12, 2022

“Why is that the goal?” Why was I reading? I was trying to prepare a meal using a new recipe, so I needed to reread it before I started. Reading was thus a subgoal. But cooking was itself a subgoal. I was cooking in order to eat, which had the goal of satisfying my hunger. So the hierarchy of goals is roughly: satisfy hunger; eat; cook; read cookbook; get more light. Wordle has been great as it has reconnected me with my best friend. Wordle has given us a connection point each day when we chat over text message. Two key aspects are that there is only one each day and that everyone is working on the same one. The limited manner gives each guess more weight and also ensures people don’t binge it and grow tired of it. The fact everyone is working on the same puzzle is what adds to the social element of the game.
