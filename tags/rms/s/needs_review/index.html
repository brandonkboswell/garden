Managing Notebooks and Documents

October 12, 2023

So you’ve started to accumulate a few notebooks and documents, and they are starting to get out of hand. Let’s talk through how we can manage and organize our documents. In this lesson, we’ll talk about creating folders and moving your notebooks around. In my opinion, the most important concept with any of these devices is to have a concept of an Archive. You need a way to make the documents you’re not actively using not clutter your space and leave you overwhelmed. In this lesson I will show you a nice simple way to do this, and in a future lesson I will show you some more exotic ways you can do this once you’ve gotten your bearings....
